This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

Last week, we put on the largest Software Carpentry bootcamp ever at the University of Washington: three rooms, six instructors, ten helpers, and 93 students spent two days together learning about Python, Git, the Unix shell, and a bunch of other useful things. Many thanks to Prof. Bill Howe for making it all possible—we look forward to coming back soon.
