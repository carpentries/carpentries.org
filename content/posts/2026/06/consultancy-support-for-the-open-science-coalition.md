Five leading open science organisations – [The Carpentries](https://carpentries.org/), [OLS](https://we-are-ols.org/), [rOpenSci](https://ropensci.org/), [pyOpenSci](https://www.pyopensci.org/), and [PREreview](https://prereview.org/) – are seeking an external consultant or group to facilitate several activities over the second half of 2026. Selected consultants will continue the organisational alignment and collaboration work [previously announced in late 2025](/blog/2025/09/convening-to-reclaim-and-sustain-open-science-communities/).

This work has three strands:

* **Facilitation**: co-ordinating between the five groups during the grant-writing period, possibly coordinating the grantwriter and governance facilitator as well.
* **Governance**: Creating ways of working between the five organisations (Pitches by invitation only). This would occur during the grant-writing period and possibly after.
* **Grantwriting**: to lead-author our multi-funder grant pitch. We expect that this grant will be pitched to multiple funders to co-fund, and may involve more than one phase, e.g. discovery phase, pilot phase, and implementation phase, over multiple years.

We would be willing to consider pitches that cover multiple roles at once.

The selected delivery partner(s) will need to coordinate with the other team(s).
