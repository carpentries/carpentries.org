## Two Organisations. One Bold Mission. 

The future of AI must be shaped by people who understand it and have the skills to question it. Today, [datocracy AI](https://www.datocracy.ai/) and [The Carpentries](https://carpentries.org/) are announcing a partnership that puts people at the very centre of the future of AI training and usage. We are proud to announce this collaboration, as a meeting of two organisations with deeply complementary missions, and a shared understanding that knowledge, when made accessible and equitable, is powerful.

## Why Our Two Organisations?

datocracy AI exists to ensure that AI and frontier technological advancements do not leave governments, social impact organisations, and small businesses in Low- and Middle-Income contexts behind. Its core belief is uncompromising - AI systems must involve the people who procure, curate, deploy, govern, use, and interpret them, and those people must be critically equipped to do so. In a world where AI adoption is accelerating faster than the understanding of AI, datocracy AI builds the capacity that makes that understanding possible.

The Carpentries have spent years doing something equally vital - lowering barriers to knowledge and making data and computational skills accessible to people around the globe. Through intensive, hands-on workshops grounded in evidence-based teaching, The Carpentries has built one of the world's most vibrant, inclusive communities of learners and peer Instructors that are highly skilled, open and collaborative. 

## What We Will Build Together

This partnership is about turning shared values into shared impact. Where one organisation's strengths meet the other's vision, something greater becomes possible, and this partnership will explore and expand on those possibilities. We will enrich an ecosystem of knowledge, expand a circle of inclusion, and amplify the voices of communities that too often remain unheard in the conversations that shape their futures. 

Together, datocracy AI and The Carpentries are planting seeds of capability that will grow into lasting change, empowering each other to go further than we could do alone. This is a partnership built to meet today's challenges and prepare for those still taking shape.

## The Moment Is Now

AI is moving fast, already reshaping institutions, research, and societies. datocracy AI and The Carpentries believe that the people most affected by this change should have the skills, the voice, and the agency to influence it on their own terms. Together, we are going to make sure that they do.
