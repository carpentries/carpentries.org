This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

We are very pleased to announce that a group of volunteers have begun translating our lessons into Spanish. If you would like to help, please head over to this GitHub repository, which has instructions and contact information. Our thanks to Fran Navarro, David Perez-Suarez, Javier J. Gutiérrez, Iván González, Daniel Domene y Zuri Bauer, and Jorge Bernabé for organizing this, and to everyone else for lending a hand.
